Trustee Information

He serves as a trustee for a notable school and is actively involved in various special initiatives for the party's advancement. His dedicated efforts have contributed significantly to the party's growth and success in the region. Mulubhai's leadership and commitment have earned him recognition and respect within the political sphere, reflecting his notable achievements and contributions to his community.

About Us

Our School, situated within the Mayur Education Campus in Nandana, caters to a diverse student body totaling approximately 1000+ students. Established decades ago, it offers education from primary to higher secondary levels. Equipped with modern facilities, including well-equipped classrooms, laboratories, a library, and sports facilities, GMDC High School aims to provide a holistic learning environment. Students eligible to attend this institution typically range from 6 to 18 years old. With a dedicated faculty and a commitment to academic excellence, GMDC High School continues to nurture and empower its students for future success.

Our Mission

Mayur Education Campus is dedicated to fostering a learning environment that liberates students to explore, create, and challenge themselves intellectually. Committed to opening minds to knowledge, we encourage students to embrace educational opportunities with respect for diverse ideas and free expression. Our mission is to instill in students a sense of joy in discovery, critical thought, and the pursuit of excellence through productive cooperation. At Mayur, we emphasize the importance of assuming responsibility for personal actions, removing constraints on full participation, and inspiring students to explore their capabilities and interests.

Our Vision

Mayur Education Campus envisions students cultivating personal and academic excellence, along with values such as tolerance, responsibility, and self-confidence, within a diverse educational environment. Our vision is centered on preparing students for lifelong learning and empowering them for effective leadership in society. Aligned with our philosophy is the belief that every child is unique, and we recognize diverse learning styles, addressing individual needs through a well-balanced curriculum and varied instructional methodologies.
